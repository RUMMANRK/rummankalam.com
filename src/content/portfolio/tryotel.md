Tryotel Travels was Saimon Global's attempt to bring a mature visa-processing business and a travel agency onto one online surface. On paper that sounds like a merge. In practice it was a harder question: what does a user actually want when they can't separate the trip from the paperwork that makes the trip possible?

The consulting work moved between strategy and execution — sitting with the chairman on direction, then sitting with engineers on build decisions. Most of what shipped came out of the basic observation that visa services and travel bookings are the same journey to the user, even if they've always been different industries to the business.
